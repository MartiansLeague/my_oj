Homework of Music

At the begin of Music class, the math teacher comes again,
and says that the Music teacher have a sick.
So he gives Nobita a math homework.

Nobita konws that for every positive integer, it can be divided into the product of some primes.
And if a number is divided into some primes, any two of them is different, Nobita says this number a handsome number.
And now Nobita wants to know there are how many number is handsome in range [1,n].

Input

The first line contains an integer n.
(1<=n<=10^14)

Output

Output one line, indicates the answer.

Sample Input

50

Sample Output

31
